Research

Marie-Curie

  • Discovered Radioactivity: Characterized and coined the term "radioactivity"

  • Discovered Elements: Identified and isolated polonium (Po) and radium (Ra)

  • Characterized Radiation:Described and studied alpha, beta, and gamma radiation

  • Developed Methods: Created techniques for isolating and purifying radioactive isotopes

  • Advanced Nuclear Physics: Contributed to the development of nuclear physics and chemistry, and applied radiation research to medicine






Aziz-Sancar

  • DNA Repair Mechanisms: Fixes DNA damage by identifying and repairing errors

  • Discovered UvrABC Enzyme: Found a key enzyme (UvrABC) that helps fix DNA

  • Elucidated DNA Repair: Figured out how DNA repair works

  • Circadian Clock and its Regulation: Studied the body's internal clock and its effects on our bodies

  • DNA Damage and Human Diseases: Explored how DNA damage affects our health and suggested new ways to treat diseases






Aziz-Sancar

  • Electroweak Unification: Proposed the Electroweak Theory, unifying electromagnetic and weak nuclear forces

  • Gauge Theories: Contributed to the development of Quantum Chromodynamics (QCD) and the Electroweak Theory

  • Symmetry and Conservation Laws: Worked on the concept of symmetry in physics, including parity and charge conjugation

  • Particle Physics: Contributed to the understanding of particle interactions and the behavior of subatomic particles

  • Cosmology: Applied theoretical physics to understand the origin and evolution of the universe
